[h1]挥金如土曼沙穆萨[/h1]

本mod对领袖曼沙·穆萨进行了加强。

本mod需要资料片：风云变幻。

[h2]调整内容[/h2]

现在曼沙穆萨可以使用信仰购买建筑、军事单位、平民单位和间谍。

沙漠丘陵也可以为外商提供+1金币。

沙漠平原改为为外商提供+2金币。

在每个回合开始时，国库的金币增加3%.


[h1]Mansa Musa the Super Purchaser[/h1]

This mod enhances the leader Mansa Musa.

This mod requires the expansion pack: "Gathering Storm".

[h2]Adjustments[/h2]

Mansa Musa can now purchase buildings, Civilian units, Military units and Spy with Faith. 

Desert Hills tile also provide +1 Gold for foreign traders. 

Flat Desert tile now provide +2 Gold for foreign traders. 

At the beginning of each turn, the Treasury gains +3% Gold.
